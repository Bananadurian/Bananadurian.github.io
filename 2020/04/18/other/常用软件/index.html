  • BitrateViewerSetup //查看视频码率

2. Test

  • SwitchHosts //host切换
  • iTest
  • Emmagee
  • easytest

3. Other

  • sourcetree //git第三方客户端
  • cmder //windowCMD窗口工具

  • xshell //SSH工具

  • fiddler //抓包工具
